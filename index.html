Help Center

Common Questions.

Edukon Academy join karne ke liye kya requirement hy?
Edukon join karne ke liye kisi professional degree ki zaroorat nahi hy. Agar aapke paas aik Laptop aur seekhne ka shoq hy, to aap hamare kisi bhi course mein enroll ho sakte hain.
Kya courses online hain ya physical?
Edukon ek digital-first academy hy. Humare tamam courses online hamari website (LMS) par available hain taake aap apni marzi ke waqt aur jagah se seekh saken.
Certificate ki verification kaise hogi?
Har certificate par aik unique ID hoti hy. Aap hamari website ke "Verify Certificate" section mein ja kar wo ID enter karenge to student ka name aur course details show ho jayengi.
Agar koi lecture samajh na aaye to kya support milegi?
Humara dedicated Discord aur WhatsApp support group 24/7 active rehta hy jahan mentors aur fellow students aapke sawalat ka jawab dete hain.
